Shakti Goap was a pioneer in fostering builder culture in India and he truly epitomised the ‘Never Stop Building’ spirit through his life’s work at Devfolio and Fold.

✦ Our story

Shakti believed that every student should, no matter their background, have the chance to build a successful life. And, fulfilling this promise depends on our country’s single most-significant lever of opportunity: a high-quality education.

To fuel his vision, we will contribute 5% of 2586 Labs’s yearly revenue in helping underprivileged, curious, and motivated students from India not only finish their formal education but also connect them with opportunities.
